這句話的意思

- `bruh` : /brə/ 非正式用語表示驚訝、不滿、或調侃語氣

- `corporately` : /ˈkɔːr.pɚ.ət.li/  `adv`
  
  - 念法比較忽略 ə  比較像是 /ˈkɔːr.pɚ.t.li/
  
  - by a large company 
  
  - acting together as a group and not as separate people
  
  - They live in a corporately owned house near the airport.
    
    - 他們住在公司擁有的房子、靠近機場。

- `mediocre` : /ˌmiː.diˈoʊ.kɚ/  `adj`
  
  - 平庸的 、not very good

- corporately accommodated mediocre people
  
  - 指企業中得到照顧的平庸的員工 ( 可看出他對被公司所容納的能力一般的員工不滿 )

> I meant that in an abstract sense of the **theoretical least** experienced programmer. I'm not bashing the practice - the less unnecessary barrier to entry the better!

  - based on the ideas that relate to a subject, not the practical uses of that subject.

- `least` :  /liːst/ `adv` 
  
  - 最少、最小
  
  - less than anything or anyone else; the smallest amount or number
  
  - I like the green one least of all.
    
    - 最不喜歡綠色那個。
  
  - He's the relative I like (the) least.

- `bash` : /bæʃ/  `verb` 
  
  - 猛擊 to hit hard 
  
  - 嚴厲批評、抨擊 to **criticize** someone **severely**. [ /səˈvɪr.li/ ][ /səˈvɪr.li/ ]

- `serverely` : /səˈvɪr.li/  `adv` 
  
  - very seriously 嚴重地
  
  - completely plainly 簡潔地 [ /ˈpleɪn.li/ ]

- `plainly` : /ˈpleɪn.li/
  
  - clearly or obviously 明顯地
  
  - simply and without a lot of decoration. [/ˌdek.ərˈeɪ.ʃən/ ]  樸素地
